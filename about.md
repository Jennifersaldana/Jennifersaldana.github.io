Hello! I'm Jennifer Saldana, a senior Computer Science student at Louisiana State University graduating in May 2026. I'm a senior at Louisiana State University, where I earned my GIAC Penetration Tester Certification (GPEN). I was given the opportunity to work with the Cybersecurity Clinic at LSU. The program was an NSA-funded project that enabled us to conduct penetration testing for small businesses across Louisiana. 

I'm interested in cybersecurity, specifically Security Operations, Threat Detection, and Incident Response. I enjoy analyzing logs, investigating suspicious behavior, and building tools to improve detection capabilities.

Through coursework and hands-on projects, I’ve built experience in penetration testing, SIEM monitoring, and log analysis. I like working in environments that simulate real-world SOC workflows and security investigations. I also conducted cybersecurity seminars for small businesses, covering how attackers utilize OSINT and the phases of a penetration test.
